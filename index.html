Who We Are?

We are a team of three web developers from Cavite State University, and our goal is to make it easy for students to rate and review the services they receive on campus. With our web app, students can simply scan a QR code to leave their feedback, making it convenient and quick to share their experiences. We believe that by gathering this information, we can not only help the university understand what students think of their services, but also identify areas for improvement. Our ultimate goal is to empower students and help the university provide the best possible experience for all students.
